Membership

Join our friendly family club on the Coventry Canal

Become a Member

Tamworth Cruising Club currently has approximately 80 members, mostly in family groups. We welcome membership applications at any time from those who share our love of the inland waterways.

Membership includes both husband and wife, as well as children up to 18 years of age - making us a truly family-friendly club.

We currently have spare non-residential mooring spaces available and welcome enquiries from prospective members. A waiting list may exist for residential moorings.

What We Expect

As a member-run club, we ask that all members participate in both the social life and the upkeep of our facilities.

Work parties are needed throughout the year to maintain the moorings, clubhouse and grounds. Many hands make light work, and these are often social occasions in themselves!

This community spirit is what makes Tamworth Cruising Club special - we all pitch in together to keep our club the wonderful place it is.

  • Participate in club social events
  • Contribute to work parties when able
  • Respect fellow members and club property
  • Help maintain our friendly, welcoming atmosphere

How to Join

1

Get in Touch

Contact our Membership Secretary, Jules Osbourne, through our contact page to express your interest and request an application form.

2

Arrange a Visit

We encourage prospective members to visit first to ensure the club is a good fit. Access is via electronic gate, so visits must be arranged by appointment.

3

Submit Application

Complete your application form and submit it to the Membership Secretary for review by the committee.

4

Welcome to the Club!

Once approved, you'll receive your membership card and access to all our facilities and events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own a boat to join?

While most of our members are boat owners, we welcome anyone with an interest in the inland waterways. Contact us to discuss your situation.

Are moorings available?

We currently have spare non-residential mooring spaces available. A waiting list may exist for residential moorings. Please enquire for current availability.

Can I visit before joining?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective members to visit first. Access is via electronic gate, so please contact us to arrange an appointment.

What are the membership fees?

For current membership fees and mooring charges, please contact our Membership Secretary who will be happy to provide details.
